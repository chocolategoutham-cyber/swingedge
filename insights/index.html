Frequently asked

What is sector rotation?

Sector rotation is the flow of capital between market groups. Some cohorts lead while others lag. The labels here are descriptive statistics, not prompts to allocate capital.

Frequently asked

What is market breadth?

Breadth summarizes how broadly stocks participate in upside or downside moves. Analysts cite it alongside price, but how you use it belongs in your trading plan.
